About SnackStop LK

Your friendly neighborhood spot for freshly made snacks.

SnackStop LK

SnackStop LK is a small local eatery that serves a wide variety of snacks and treats loved by everyone in one cozy spot. From sweet treats to savoury bites, our menu blends the best of Sri Lankan favourites with classic snacks like cakes, hot dogs, rolls and many more, all freshly made and affordably priced. Whether you're popping in for a quick bite, picking up an order, or getting food delivered to your doorstep, we make it easy and convenient. We also take customized orders for small events and special occasions. Open to anyone and everyone, SnackStop LK is your friendly go-to spot for affordable, freshly made snacks, anytime you're hungry.

How We Started

SnackStop LK began with a simple love for good food and a desire to share it with others. What started as a few homemade snacks for friends and family quickly turned into a small eatery loved by many.

What We Believe In

We believe in keeping things fresh, affordable, and made with care. From the ingredients we choose to the service we offer, our goal is to make sure every customer leaves with a smile.

Who We Serve

Our doors are open to everyone; students, workers, families, and anyone who enjoys a tasty bite. Whether you're in a rush, planning a party, or just craving something local and satisfying, we've got something for you.
